# Kotak Cashback+ Product Pack

Date: 2026-03-27

## Product Summary
Kotak Cashback+ is positioned as a practical, savings-led everyday cashback credit card for digitally active metro users. The product value is strongest when the card is framed around repeatable monthly categories rather than luxury or aspirational spending.

## Audience
User-provided audience definition:
- Aspirational NCCS A/B Indians in top metro cities
- Majorly salaried, with some self-employed users
- Age 21 to 40
- Value-driven, digitally active, savings-oriented
- Practical, clarity-seeking, and utility-first

## Core RTBs To Use
- 5% cashback on daily essentials
- 5% cashback on entertainment
- 5% cashback on groceries and food delivery
- Up to 4% fuel benefits
- 0 joining fee / first-year-free only as a limited-period promotional claim

## Official Product Facts
- Main product headline: up to 5% cashback on spends, with a limited-time first-year-free offer
- Accelerated cashback: 5% on online food delivery and groceries
- Accelerated cashback: 5% on online entertainment
- Fuel value: 3% cashback on fuel plus 1% fuel surcharge waiver
- Base earn rate: unlimited 0.5% cashback on other eligible spends
- Accelerated cashback cap: 750 points per billing cycle
- Redemption: 1 reward point = Rs 1, redeemed as cashback against statement outstanding
- Redemption threshold: reward points redeemed in multiples of 400
- Expiry: unclaimed cashback expires after 1 year
- Standard fee table: joining fee Rs 750 plus GST, annual fee Rs 750 plus GST
- Annual fee waiver: annual retail spends of Rs 2 lakh
- Fuel surcharge waiver band: transactions between Rs 500 and Rs 4000, capped at Rs 3500 in an anniversary year
- Restricted categories: rent, B2B, utilities, insurance, education and government, wallet, online skill-based gaming, EMI
- Add-on cards: up to 3 add-on cards, free of cost
- Entertainment offer: PVR INOX Buy 1 Get 1, max discount Rs 250 per month, shown valid till 31 March 2026
- Published value chart: annual modeled benefit of Rs 10,410 on annual spends of Rs 2,16,000

## Claim Guardrails
- Do not say cashback on everything
- Do not treat 0 joining fee as a permanent fee fact; frame it as limited-time / first-year-free promotional messaging
- Do not say flat 4% fuel cashback; frame it as up to 4% fuel benefits or 3% cashback plus 1% surcharge waiver
- Keep 5% cashback tied to online grocery, food delivery, and online entertainment
- Offline grocery, dining, and entertainment should not be framed as accelerated cashback
